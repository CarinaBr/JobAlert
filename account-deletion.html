Account Deletion Request

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please send an email to:

couplevisionplanner@protonmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion via email.

All your data including profile information, visions, milestones, lists, and collections will be permanently deleted.

Warning: If you have connected your space with your partner and created shared Visions, Milestones, Lists, Tasks or Collection Segments and Items, these cannot be deleted by us as this is a shared space. We will delete the personal data that you provided to us (profile picture, name, e-mail address, password).

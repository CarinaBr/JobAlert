1. Data We Collect

Account Data

  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely using hashing)
  • Profile name
  • Profile picture (optional)

User-Generated Content

  • Visions (your life goals and plans)
  • Milestones and tasks
  • Lists (to-do, shopping, etc.)
  • Collections (movies, restaurants, bucket list items, etc.)

Financial Planning Data

You may voluntarily enter savings goals and amounts to track your progress. This data is not connected to any bank accounts and is used solely for display purposes within the app.

Device Permissions

  • Camera: Used only to take profile photos. Photos are stored locally on your device or uploaded to our secure servers if you choose to save them.

2. How We Store Your Data

Cloud Storage

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore database and Firebase Authentication). Data is encrypted:

  • At rest: AES-256 encryption
  • In transit: TLS 1.2+ encryption

Local Storage

Some data may be cached locally on your device for offline access. This cache is cleared when you log out.

3. Partner Data & Shared Spaces

Before Partner Connection

You can enter a name for your partner. This is a free-text field and is not verified. It is used only to personalise your app experience.

After Partner Connection

When your partner creates their own account and accepts your invitation to join a shared Space, they consent to our Privacy Policy themselves. From that point, content in the shared Space is visible to both partners.

4. How We Share Your Data

With Your Partner

Content in your shared Space is visible to your connected partner.

Service Providers

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage) - Privacy Policy
  • Expo (app infrastructure) - Privacy Policy

We Do NOT

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Active Accounts

Your data is stored as long as your account is active.

Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Deleted: Your email, password, profile picture, and display name
  • Retained: Content in shared Spaces remains accessible to your partner
  • Task assignments are set to "unassigned"

Your Responsibility

If you have entered sensitive information in free-text fields (e.g., passwords, personal notes), please delete this content yourself before leaving a Space or deleting your account.
